Supported browsers

The Free Trade Agreement Portal has been developed for modern browsers, including:

  • Chrome 43 and above
  • Firefox 39 and above
  • Safari 8 and above
  • Internet Explorer 10 and above

The FTA Portal supports other recent browsers through to the following version, although there may be usability issues:

  • Chrome 36
  • Firefox 38
  • Safari 7
  • Internet Explorer 9.

Access to, and the usability of the site through other older versions is not guaranteed.

Responsive access

The first release of the FTA Portal has not been optimised for mobile and tablet access.

Users of such devices can still access the site but its usability is not guaranteed. A mobile responsive version of the site will be available in a future release.
