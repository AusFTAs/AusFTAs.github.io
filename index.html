About this website

Browser support

The FTA Portal has been developed to fully support modern browsers including:

  • Chrome 43 and above
  • Firefox 39 and above
  • Safari 8 and above
  • Internet Explorer 10 and above

The FTA Portal supports other recent browsers (although there may be usability issues) through to:

  • Chrome 36
  • Firefox 38
  • Safari 7
  • Internet Explorer 9

Access to, and the usability of the site through other older browser versions not guaranteed.

Users with earlier browser versions will be provided with a message to remind them that the browser is out of date and it should be updated it for better security and the best experience on this site.

Responsive access

The initial release of the FTA Portal has not been optimised for mobile and tablet access. Users of such devices can still access the site but the usability of the site is not guaranteed. A mobile responsive version of the site will be available in a future release.

Publisher

The FTA Portal is managed by the Free Trade Agreement Division of the Department of Foreign Affairs and Trade (DFAT). The project has been supported by other Australian Government agencies including Austrade, Department of Immigration and Border Protection, Department of Industry and Science and Department of Agriculture.

A number of peak industry associations and state and territory governments have also provided valuable advice on the requirements and features of the site.

We welcome your feedback on the FTA Portal project.
